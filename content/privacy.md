## Quick summary

Moniemind is a content website. We do not require accounts, do not store credit-card information, do not handle your money, and do not sell personal data. Below is the full breakdown.

## Who we are

**Moniemind** (moniemind.com), operated by Augustine Usifo, Lagos, Nigeria.
Privacy contact: **info@moniemind.com**

## What we collect

### 1. Anonymous analytics
If we use a privacy-respecting analytics tool, it logs:
- Pages visited
- General location (country / region — never street-level)
- Browser type and screen size
- Referring website

This data is **aggregated and anonymous**. It cannot be tied back to your identity.

### 2. Affiliate-link click attribution
When you click an affiliate link to a fintech platform (e.g. Bamboo, Risevest), the destination sets a tracking cookie or referrer parameter identifying Moniemind as the referrer. **We do not see your sign-up details, deposit amounts, or any account information** — only that a referral credit occurred.

The fintech platform's privacy policy governs everything that happens after you click.

### 3. Voluntary contact
If you email us at info@moniemind.com, we receive your email and any info you choose to include. We keep emails for support purposes only and never share them with third parties.

⚠ **Please never send us your account numbers, BVN, NIN, full card details, or trading account credentials.** We don't need this information and email is not a secure channel for it.

## What we do NOT collect

- We do not have user accounts or logins on this site.
- We do not store financial-account information (sign-ups happen on platform sites, not here).
- We do not run intake forms, KYC processes, or any kind of financial onboarding.
- We do not share, sell, or otherwise transfer your data to third parties.

## Your rights

Under the Nigerian **NDPR**, the EU **GDPR**, the UK **DPA**, and similar laws:

- **Access:** You may ask what data we hold on you. Reply within 30 days.
- **Deletion:** You may ask us to delete data we hold. Done within 30 days unless legal retention applies.
- **Opt-out of analytics:** Most browsers offer a "Do Not Track" signal — we honor it. Browser extensions like uBlock Origin or Privacy Badger also work.
- **Cookie control:** Clear or block cookies in your browser settings.

To exercise any right, email **info@moniemind.com** with subject "Privacy request".

## Children

Moniemind is not directed at children under 18 (consistent with regulatory restrictions on financial services advertising to minors). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

## Fraud prevention notice

If we ever observe credible evidence that someone is misusing the site or our content for fraudulent purposes (e.g. fake offshoots scraping our articles and adding scam referral links), we may share relevant information with **NFIU**, **EFCC**, **SEC Nigeria**, or **CBN** in line with their lawful information-sharing requirements. We will not share user data outside of these legally-compelled situations.

## Changes to this policy

We date-stamp this page whenever we update it. Material changes will be flagged at the top for 30 days.
