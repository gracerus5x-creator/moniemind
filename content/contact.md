## Primary inbox

**info@moniemind.com**

This goes to the editorial team. Reply within **2 business days** (Lagos time, UTC+1).

## What to send where

| What you have | Use |
|---|---|
| **Correction or update** on a platform review (fee change, new feature, downtime issue) | info@moniemind.com — subject: "Correction: [platform / article]" |
| **Story tip** — regulatory news, new product, platform incident | info@moniemind.com — subject: "Tip: [topic]" |
| **Platform partnership / affiliate inquiry** | info@moniemind.com — subject: "Partnership" |
| **Press / interview / podcast request** | info@moniemind.com — subject: "Press" |
| **Privacy concern or data-deletion request** | info@moniemind.com — subject: "Privacy" (see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/)) |

## What we cannot help with

- **Personal investment advice.** We are not licensed financial advisors. We cannot tell you whether to buy a specific stock, how to allocate your portfolio, or whether a specific platform is right for your individual circumstances.
- **Diagnosing problems with specific platform accounts** — please contact the platform's support directly (we'll publish the contact info if a platform makes it hard to find).
- **Tax advice.** Please consult a Nigerian-licensed tax professional.

## Office location

Operated remotely from **Lagos, Nigeria** (West Africa, UTC+1).

## Response time expectations

- **Editorial corrections:** within 2 business days, fixes within 48 hours of confirmation
- **Privacy / data-deletion requests:** within 30 days (legal max under NDPR/GDPR)
- **Partnership / affiliate proposals:** within 5 business days

## Scam / fraud alerts

If you have evidence of a Nigerian fintech scam, a Ponzi scheme operating, or a platform we cover engaging in deceptive practices — please share it. We take fraud reports seriously and will investigate and publish where appropriate. Send to info@moniemind.com with subject "Fraud report".

For active fraud against you personally, also report to:
- **Nigeria Financial Intelligence Unit (NFIU)**
- **EFCC** (Economic and Financial Crimes Commission)
- The relevant **SEC Nigeria** complaints channel if it involves securities
