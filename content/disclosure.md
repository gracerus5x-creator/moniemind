## Plain-English summary

**Some platform links on Moniemind are affiliate / referral links.** When you click one and sign up (and for some platforms, fund your account), we may earn a referral commission — at **no extra cost to you**. In some cases you actually receive a bonus too (Bamboo new-user bonus, etc).

This disclosure complies with the FTC, ASA, FCCPC (Nigeria), and similar consumer-protection bodies internationally.

## Programs we participate in

Moniemind is a participant in (or has applied to):

- **Bamboo** (direct referral — Play Store referrer link)
- **Risevest** (direct referral — /ref/ URL pattern)
- **Trove, PiggyVest** (pending / pending direct partnership)
- **Binance** (revenue-share affiliate)
- **eToro** (CPA on funded accounts)
- **Impact.com & ShareASale** — for fintech-adjacent SaaS, books, and tools

Cowrywise is mentioned in articles but is **not an affiliate partner** — their referral program is fee-waiver only, not cash, so we treat coverage as purely editorial.

## How affiliate commission affects our coverage

It **does not affect our ratings**:

1. We pick platforms to review based on what readers ask about — not commission rates.
2. When we compare platforms, we compare on **real costs to the user**: deposit fees, withdrawal fees, FX spreads, hidden charges, regulatory status, downtime history.
3. We recommend the cheapest / best-fit option for the use case — even if it pays the lowest commission, or none.
4. If a higher-commission platform has a known fraud incident, customer-service collapse, or regulatory action, those go IN the article — not buried.

## iOS vs Android caveat

Some Nigerian fintech apps (e.g. Bamboo) use Play Store referrer parameters that **only work on Android**. iOS users who click those links won't auto-credit our referral — and won't receive the new-user bonus either. Where this applies, our articles spell out the iOS workaround (enter referral code manually at signup).

## What we will never do

- Promote a platform we wouldn't use ourselves.
- Promise specific investment returns.
- Hide negative platform information (downtime, fees, customer-service collapse) to protect commission.
- Disguise paid placements as editorial.
- Promote unlicensed or unregulated investment products.

## Not financial advice

Affiliate or not, **nothing on this site constitutes regulated financial advice**. Investments carry the risk of partial or total loss. Past performance does not predict future returns. Always do your own research and consider consulting a licensed Nigerian financial advisor before committing significant sums.

## Cookie-based tracking

When you click an affiliate link, the destination platform may set a tracking cookie or attach a referral parameter identifying Moniemind as the referrer. Some referrals (Play Store URLs) attribute via Google Play install metadata rather than browser cookies. We do not see your transaction details — only commission credit.
